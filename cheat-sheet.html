Founder's Pitch Cheat Sheet
Say this first
"We're not an agency. We're a technology company that grows D2C brands."
The pitch

10 seconds: "We help D2C brands grow revenue or cut costs using AI and data, and we build the tech ourselves, so we go well beyond a normal agency."

30 seconds: "Most brands don't have a marketing problem, they have a systems problem. We're an Ex Amazon, Duke trained founder's team of engineers and marketers. We do web, performance marketing, marketplaces and social, then build AI agents, unify your data and add 3D commerce on top. 150+ brands, including Jiiva and Nivetha Pethuraj."

Open with a question
  • "If I asked your best selling product in Chennai yesterday, could you tell me in 10 seconds?"
  • "Who answers your customer calls and DMs right now?"
  • "Does your store look like the other 400,000 Shopify stores, or make people stop?"
  • When they pause or laugh, that's your wedge.
Always say the benefit, not the service
Performance marketing→More sales for less ad spend. 3.8x ROAS on managed spend.
AI agents→Calls and DMs answered 24/7 by an agent customers can't tell from a human.
Unified data→Any number, by city, product or day, answered in seconds, not a meeting.
Web development→A store people play with: 3D, tools and games, so browsers become buyers.
Always ahead→When Google ships Universal Cart this year, you'll already be set up for it.
Handle objections
"We already have an agency." Keep them for the basics. We're the tech layer they can't build: AI agents, data, custom tools. We often work alongside agencies. "Too expensive / we're small." Big or small, we start with one fix that pays for itself, usually lower cost per order or hours saved. "AI sounds complicated." You don't touch it. We built a 50+ founder a custom CRM in minutes. Today he runs it himself.
Close with a small yes
"Give me 20 minutes next week, I'll pull your category and show you the easy wins. Tuesday or Thursday?"

No pressure version: "Scan this and I'll send you a free one page teardown of your store and where you're losing money."
Soundbites to repeat
We don't just manage brands. We fix what's broken. Not a marketing problem, a systems problem. We bring the tech. You get the results. You don't have to break your head. Grow revenue, cut costs, or both. Ready for what's next, before your competitors notice.
